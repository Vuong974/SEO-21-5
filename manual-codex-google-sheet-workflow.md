# Workflow thu cong trong Codex cho Google Sheet tu khoa

## Muc tieu

Moi lan ban them du lieu moi vao Google Sheet, ban chi can nhan cho Codex mot cau lenh ngan. Codex se mo file Google Sheet, tim cac dong moi chua duoc phan tich, phan loai keyword SEO, roi ghi ket qua vao cac cot dau ra.

Workflow nay khong chay nen 24/7. No chi chay khi ban yeu cau Codex.

## Google Sheet mac dinh

File hien tai:

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bsvjSeM-_9_KJZkJ-TD4tJ4PlDzt8eWgMJI9vpjdEBo/edit

Tab nguon mac dinh:

`Tu Khoa Da Xu Ly`

Neu ten tab trong Google Sheet co dau tieng Viet, Codex se dung dung ten tab thuc te:

`Từ Khóa Đã Xử Lý`

## Cot dau vao

Codex se doc cac cot:

| Cot | Ten cot |
| --- | --- |
| A | Query |
| B | Clicks |
| C | Impressions |
| D | CTR |
| E | Average Position |

Neu co cot `Tiêu chuẩn/Nhóm` da co san, Codex co the dung lam ngu canh. Neu cot nay trong, Codex se tu phan loai.

## Cach Codex nhan dien dong moi

Mot dong duoc xem la can xu ly khi:

1. Cot `Query` co du lieu.
2. Mot trong cac cot dau ra con trong, thuong la `Tiêu chuẩn/Nhóm` hoac `Search Intent`.

Codex se khong xu ly lai cac dong da co ket qua day du, tru khi ban yeu cau.

## Cau lenh mau de chay

Ban chi can nhan:

```text
Chay workflow phan tich keyword moi trong Google Sheet:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1bsvjSeM-_9_KJZkJ-TD4tJ4PlDzt8eWgMJI9vpjdEBo/edit

Hay doc tab `Từ Khóa Đã Xử Lý`, tim cac dong co Query nhung cot dau ra con trong, phan tich theo skill classify-seo-keywords, roi ghi ket qua vao cac cot:
Tiêu chuẩn/Nhóm, Search Intent, Funnel Stage, Primary Keyword, Ghi chú.

Chi xu ly dong moi, khong sua cac dong da co ket qua.
```

Ban cung co the dung cau ngan hon:

```text
Chay workflow phan tich cac keyword moi trong sheet KNA.
```

## Quy tac phan tich

Codex se uu tien cac nhan sau:

### Search Intent

- `Informational`
- `Commercial`
- `Transactional`
- `Navigational`
- `Local`
- `Support`

### Funnel Stage

- `TOFU`
- `MOFU`
- `BOFU`
- `Retention`

### Primary Keyword

- `Yes` neu la keyword dai dien cho mot trang/cum noi dung chinh.
- `No` neu la bien the, long-tail ho tro, FAQ, hoac keyword phu.

### Ghi chu

Dung de giai thich ngan gon ly do phan loai, vi du:

- `Intent ro, phu hop landing page dich vu`
- `Long-tail ho tro`
- `Can kiem tra SERP`
- `Keyword gan voi dao tao/khoa hoc`
- `Rui ro trung intent voi keyword chinh`

## Quy trinh Codex se thuc hien

1. Mo Google Sheet bang connector.
2. Doc metadata de xac dinh dung tab va cot.
3. Tim cac dong moi chua co ket qua.
4. Phan tich theo bo tieu chuan/nganh cua KNA.
5. Ghi ket qua vao dung cot tren cung dong.
6. Kiem tra lai mot mau dong sau khi ghi.
7. Bao cao so dong da xu ly va cac dong can ban ra soat neu co.

## Luu y van hanh

- Nen them du lieu moi vao cuoi tab nguon.
- Nen giu ten cot on dinh.
- Neu them cot moi, hay bao Codex truoc khi chay workflow.
- Neu muon phan tich lai toan bo sheet, hay noi ro: `phan tich lai toan bo`, vi mac dinh Codex chi xu ly dong moi.
